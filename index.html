  • Multiple Format Support: Resize photos for PAN cards, passports, Aadhaar cards, and more
  • Signature Resizing: Optimize signatures for PAN card and bank applications
  • Document Processing: Handle A4 and letter-size document formatting
  • Advanced Editing: Crop, adjust brightness, contrast, and zoom functionality
  • Client-Side Processing: All processing happens in your browser for maximum privacy
  • Multiple Output Formats: Save as JPEG or PDF based on requirements

PAN Card Photo Requirements

For PAN card applications, your photograph must meet specific requirements:

  • Size: 3.5cm x 4.5cm (200x240 pixels)
  • Format: JPEG
  • File size: Less than 50KB for online applications
  • Background: Light colored, preferably white
  • Quality: Clear and recent photograph
